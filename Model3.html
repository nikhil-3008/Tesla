Special Features



DIGITAL LED HEADLIGHTS

Greater visibility, safety and resolution means you see more at night. The Digital LED headlights with signature DRL, Image Projection and Adaptive Front Lighting use navigation data to pre-emptively follow the road ahead.


3D SURROUND CAMERA

Included with 3D Surround Camera, ClearSight Ground View6 effectively lets you see ‘through' the bonnet, while manoeuvring lights illuminate the ground at low speed to help guide you safely into tight spots.


CLEARSIGHT INTERIOR REAR VIEW MIRROR

This feature5 provides an unobstructed view of the road behind you. A camera, located in the antenna, feeds live video to your rear view mirror, ensuring constant visibility regardless of rear-seat passengers or large objects in the loadspace.
